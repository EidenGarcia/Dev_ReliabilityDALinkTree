Estimation Tool Disclaimer

This tool is an estimation calculator for the operating time of overcurrent protection devices using ANSI standard curves (S&C IntelliRupter), S&C TripSaver II K-Speed fuse link TCC curves, S&C Power Fuses Type SMU-20 tabulated TCC data (TCC 165-2 & 165-2-2), the ABB DPU2000R ANSI Time Overcurrent characteristic curves, GE Multilin 350 5-constant ANSI curves, and ABB RE620 ANSI/IEC IDMT curves.

🚫 This calculator must NOT be used as the basis for any final engineering determination, protection coordination study, fuse sizing, or equipment design decision. Results are approximate estimates intended solely as preliminary guidance. Always verify using certified relay-coordination software and consult a qualified protection engineer before any operational or design decision.
